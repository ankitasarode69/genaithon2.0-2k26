    Hackathon Rules

    1
    Each team must have 1–4 members.
    2
    Teams must register before the deadline using the registration form.
    3
    Each team must submit a PPT of their project idea before 16 March for Round 1 selection.
    4
    The Round 2 presentation will be held online on 29 March in front of judges.
    5
    Only 10 teams will be shortlisted for the final round.
    6
    The final round will be held on campus on 11 April.
    7
    Teams must bring their own laptops and required software/tools.
    8
    All projects must be original and innovative.
    9
    Judges' decisions will be final and binding.
    10
    The top 3 teams will receive exciting cash prizes and certificates.
